Bringing powerful automation and seamless integration directly into your browser workflow.

Work Smarter, Not Harder.

Boost your team's efficiency where they work the most—the browser. We create smart Chrome extensions that automate repetitive tasks, integrate with your core applications, and put powerful tools right at your fingertips, saving hours of manual effort and reducing human error.

Key Benefits of Custom Extensions

Task Automation

Automate data entry, form filling, web scraping, and other repetitive browser-based tasks to free up your team for more valuable work.

Seamless Workflow Integration

Connect your browser directly to your internal tools, CRM, or project management software, creating a unified and efficient workflow.

Instant Data Accessibility

Overlay contextual information from your internal systems onto any webpage, giving your team the data they need, when they need it.

Improved User Experience

Simplify complex third-party websites or add new functionality to your own web applications, making them easier and faster to use.

Our Extension Development Process

We build secure, reliable, and intuitive extensions that deliver immediate value to your team.

  1. Workflow Analysis: We start by analyzing your team's current browser-based workflows to identify the biggest bottlenecks and opportunities for automation.
  2. UI/UX & Prototyping: We design a simple and intuitive user interface for the extension, ensuring it's easy to use and integrates naturally into the browser.
  3. Development & Integration: Our team develops the core functionality and integrates it securely with any required internal or external APIs.
  4. Testing & Deployment: We rigorously test the extension for performance and security before helping you deploy it privately to your organization's Chrome users.
