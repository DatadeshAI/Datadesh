Our Dashboard Development Process

We build dashboards that are not only visually appealing but also strategically designed to answer your most important business questions.

  1. Requirement Gathering: We begin by understanding your key business objectives and the KPIs you need to track to define the scope and purpose of the dashboard.
  2. Data Modeling & Integration: We connect to your data sources (Excel, SQL, cloud services, etc.) and build a robust, optimized data model to ensure fast and accurate reporting.
  3. Dashboard Design & Development: Our team designs and develops intuitive and insightful reports with compelling visuals, tailored to your audience.
  4. Deployment & Training: We publish the dashboards to the Power BI service, set up refresh schedules, and provide training to ensure your team can fully leverage their new tool.
