How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

1. What are Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data that a website asks your browser to store on your computer or mobile device. The cookie allows the website to "remember" your actions or preferences over time.

2. Why do we use Cookies?

Datadesh uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and serve personalized content. Our use of cookies falls into several categories:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the site.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use a website, for instance, which pages visitors go to most often. These cookies don't collect information that identifies a visitor.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your user name or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

3. Managing your preferences

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Updates to this policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our cookie practices.
