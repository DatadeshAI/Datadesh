Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Datadesh, we are committed to making our digital products and services accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. We believe that technology should empower all users, and we strive to provide an inclusive experience for all visitors to our website.

1. Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with visual, hearing, cognitive, and motor impairments.

2. Accessibility Features

We implement a variety of features to improve accessibility, including:

  • Semantic HTML: Proper use of headings, lists, and landmarks to aid screen reader navigation.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Ensuring all interactive elements are reachable and usable via keyboard alone.
  • Alternative Text: Providing descriptive alt text for meaningful images and icons.
  • Color Contrast: Maintaining high contrast ratios between text and background colors for improved legibility.

3. Ongoing Monitoring

Accessibility is a continuous journey. We regularly review our website using both automated tools and manual testing to identify and address potential barriers to accessibility.

4. Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Datadesh website. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about accessibility, please reach out to us at datadeshai@gmail.com. We will make every reasonable effort to accommodate your needs.
